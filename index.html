Case Studies

Real-world examples of how pragmatic, reliability-first thinking solves critical business challenges.

Data Center Exit Under Austerity

Challenge: Exit a closing data center in 12 months with undocumented, physical servers.

Action: Led the reverse-engineering and P2V migration of all critical systems to a private cloud.

Result: Completed the migration ahead of schedule with zero revenue loss, transforming high-risk hardware into manageable virtual assets.

AWS Migration & Modernization

Challenge: Migrate an entire platform from a bottlenecked hosting provider to AWS in under 6 months.

Action: Planned and executed a "lift and shift" migration, managing consultants and redesigning the observability stack for the cloud.

Result: Completed the migration with zero downtime, unlocking new capabilities and leading to an AWS case study feature.

Platform Consolidation & Revenue Protection

Challenge: Integrate a legacy tracking system (250M monthly requests) into a new platform with zero customer impact.

Action: Co-designed a multi-layer reverse proxy architecture using Cloudflare and AWS Lambda for intelligent, seamless traffic routing.

Result: Protected 99.5% of channel revenue during the migration and reduced error rates by 75%.
